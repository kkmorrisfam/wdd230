The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan for

Crescent City, California

Site Name

Crescent City Chamber of Commerce: This name represents a city in which the chamber operates

Site Purpose

The Crescent City Chamber of Commerce is an association of businesses. It's purpose is to provide a network for local business as well as resources for business to be successful. It should also provide information to become a member.

Scenarios

What kinds of questions might be asked by a site visitor?

  • Where are you located?
  • How can I become a member?
  • What does Crescent City provide to visitors or residents?
  • What events are coming up where members can network?
  • Where can I find contact information?
  • Who is incharge?
  • What do I get for being a member?
  • What is the population base for the city or greater-area?

Color Schema

I will use the Hunter Green for the navigation bar and the h1 & h2 elements. I will use the Hunyadi Yellow with the contrast for the navigation items. I will use the other colors for background colors in different sections.

  • 03256C - Royal Blue (White)
  • 44CFCB - Robin Egg Blue (Black)
  • 3F6634 - Hunter Green (White)
  • EDAE49 - Hunyadi Yellow (Black)
  • 452103 - Seal Brown (White)

The color in the paranthesis represents the best text color in combination with this color.
