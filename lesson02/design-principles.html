PARC: Contrast

Land-O-Lakes

landolakes.com

The Contrast principle on this website page is used to emphasize the differences in each element thereby making each one stand out. I particularly like the red background in the second section next to the third section with the red in the strawberries and tomatoes against contrasting colors. This is pleasing to the eye and adds to the readability of the page. It also directs users to either click on either of the receipe buttons.

Fitt's Law

Chase Bank

chase.com

The Chase Bank website utilizes Fitt’s Law by making important elements, such as buttons and links, larger and placing them within easy reach. This helps minimize the time and effort a user needs to find what they need on the site. It’s easy to see where to open an account, or learn about other bank activities as well as login to a user account. It fulfils its purpose for a website by doing this.

White Space and Clean Design

OttLite

ottlite.com

The OttLite website uses the White Space and Clean Design principles. It has empty space around the second and third sections to give visual breathing room and create a clean look. This enhances readability and reduces visual clutter. The one image at the top is highlighted and the rest of the page is organized and minimal to find things easily. The top section has all of the images, but they run in a slide show presentation and are not all over the page.
