Explore Cozumel with ~Scoots~

Your Ultimate Source for Motor Scooter Adventures on the Island

~Scoots~ offers a variety of rental options to cater to different preferences and group sizes. For solo travelers or couples, they provide Honda Motor Scooters, including the Honda Metropolitan (49cc) suitable for one person, the Honda Dio (110cc) accommodating two persons, and the Honda PCX150 (149cc) also suitable for two riders. For those looking for more adventurous options, the company offers the Honda Pioneer 1000 side-by-side ATVs, which can accommodate up to four persons. Additionally, they provide Jeep rentals, with options including a Jeep Wrangler 4-door manual with A/C, accommodating five persons, and a Jeep Wrangler 2-door open-air manual suitable for four persons.

To confirm a reservation, a $50 security deposit is required, and renters must present a valid driver's license at the time of rental. Riders must be at least 12 years old to use the scooters. While the fuel charge is not included in the rental fee, unlimited mileage is offered, and liability insurance is included. Renters can also opt to purchase damage insurance. For the convenience of customers, free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs is available for rentals of two days or more. Payments can be made via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo. Additionally, rentals can be returned to either of the company's two physical locations.
