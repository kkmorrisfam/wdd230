“We value the Chamber’s orientation toward travel and tourism which emphasizes recruiting our 'next visitor'. Del Norte County is an extraordinary destination with uncommon attractions. Our beautiful beaches and rugged coastline, Wild and Scenic Rivers, Redwood National and State Parks, and the backcountry lands of the vast Smith River National Recreation Area provide unmatched recreation and tourism opportunities. We’re grateful to the Chamber as a community leader and partner on the celebration of these world-class natural resources.”
